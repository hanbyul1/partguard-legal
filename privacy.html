Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

PartGuard is designed to respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Data Collection

PartGuard does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data processing is performed locally on your device.

This app does not use tracking technologies or analytics services.

Camera and Photos Access

The app may request access to your camera or photo library solely for the purpose of scanning or importing repair receipts. These images are processed only on-device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

PartGuard may access publicly available data sources (such as automotive part pricing or diagnostic datasets), but does not transmit user data to these services.

Data Security

Since all processing occurs on-device, your data remains under your control and is not stored externally.
